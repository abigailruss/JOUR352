What is the difference between your <head> and <body> tags?

Head tag stores metadata and body tag shows whats visible on the page. Head holds links to css and google fonts and the title. Things you can't see go in the head. Things you can see go in the body.

  • meta: used for SEO (title, description,keywords), used for responsiveness to help resizing(viewport)
  • title: its on the tab of your browser. The title helps for seo.

Formatting and validating

What is the value of formatting your code?

Readability. Clarity of code. Easier for others to understand what is happening in your code. See whats nested. Find mistakes/debug your code.

Free formater is a good page for letting it be formatted for you

Why is it especially important to have clean, readable CSS code?

Cascading style sheets: what happenes at the top drips down to whatever happens throughout the sheet. Having 2 h1 selectors will mean the second one overrides the first one. It will take whatever is last so its very important to have clean, well organized and readable stylesheets. Styles written at the top won't be overwritten if one selector is more specific than the general one. The more specific you get the harder it will be for you to override yourself further down.

specificity is key!
